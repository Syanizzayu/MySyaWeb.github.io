My Experience

Explore the key highlights of my professional journey and achievements.

Part-time worker at Watsons

As a part-time worker at Watson, I have been working with dedication even though I faced a lot of obstacles. For me, that is not a reason why I need to hate my job. I have been working for two months part-time before pursuing my education journey. My job scope is customer service, where I need to serve and fulfill the needs of customers. It is not that easy to handle a lot of people and different attitudes for each of them. Other than that, without realizing it, my communication skills have improved since I talk with many customers. 

Waiter

As a waiter at Dapoqkakna restaurant during my semester breaks for three months. My job scope is serving food and beverages to the customers, and it is also my responsibility to ensure the cleanliness of the restaurant. Other than that, my priority is to ensure the satisfaction of the customers and their needs.Furthermore, I am a trusted person because I used to become a cashier and handle money. During my time as a cashier,  no issues appeared regarding the money.While working as a waiter, I have improved my multitasking skills because I did a lot of jobs at the same time.

Involved as member of SULAM program

During my third semester, I was involved in joining a program of SULAM as a member under my curriculum activities. I took part as a member of the Special Task Force Bureau. My job scope is being responsible for taking on other members' tasks when they need help. I have been working in a team to ensure the program is going well. As a member, I need to be responsible for the task that has been given to me. Since joining the program, my communication and working in team skills have enhanced because I no longer talk with strangers.
